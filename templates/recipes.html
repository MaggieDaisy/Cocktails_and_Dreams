Sours Margarita Show Recipe
Margarita Close Recipe

A margarita is a cocktail consisting of tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice often served with salt on the rim of the glass. The drink is served shaken with ice, blended with ice, or without ice.

Cointreau 1 shot, Lime juice 1 shot, Tequila 2 shots

Rub the rim of the glass with the lime slice to make the salt stick to it. Shake the other ingredients with ice, then carefully pour into the glass. Garnish with lime slice and serve over ice.

Margarita Glass, Shaker

Old-Fashioned Old Fashion Show Recipe
Old Fashioned Close Recipe

The Old Fashioned represents an essential chapter in cocktail history & when made properly deserves its place amongst the great cocktails. This recipe perfectly balances boozey, with sweet, fruity & bitter in a nuanced combination of ingredients that just never gets old. Feel free to try Rye Whiskey, Cognac, or Rum in place of Bourbon in a nod to other "old-fashioned" cocktails.

Bourbon Whiskey 2shots, Simple Sugar Syrup 1tsp, Angostura Bitters 2dashes

Build the whiskey, simple syrup & bitters into a mixing glass with ice & stir. Strain over fresh ice into an old-fashioned glass, serve on the rocks. Twist the orange peel over the surface of the cocktail, & then rub the rim of the glass with the orange peel before dropping in to serve. Garnish with Cocktail cherry, Orange slice, Orange peel

Old Fashioned Glass, Mixing Glass, Stirring Spoon, Citrus Peeler

Fizz or Collins Mojito Show Recipe
Mojito Close Recipe

The refreshingly minty-citrus flavours of Cuba's Mojito cocktail perfectly compliment white Rum & is wonderful on a hot summer's day. Highly popular, yet tricky to get right & often served too sweet - the key to a good Mojito is to use plenty of fresh Mint & Lime juice but not too much Sugar.

White Rum 2shots, Sugar Syrup 0.5shot, Lime Wedges 4, Fresh Mint12 leaves, Soda Waterfill to the top

Place the Mint, Sugar Syrup & Lime wedges into a highball glass & lightly muddle the ingredients together. The Lime wedges & Mint leaves should be bruised to release their juices & essential oils. Fill the glass with crushed ice, pour over the White Rum & stir. Top up with Soda Water & stir well from the bottom up. Garnish with a sprig of Mint & serve with a straw.

Copper Mug, Ice Crusher, Muddler, Stirring Spoon, Stainless Steel Straws

Three-parters Espresso Martini Show Recipe
Espresso Martini Close Recipe

The espresso martini is a cold, coffee-flavored cocktail made with vodka, espresso coffee, coffee liqueur, and sugar syrup. It is not a true martini as it contains neither gin nor vermouth, but is one of many drinks that incorporate the term into their names.

Vodka1.3 shots, Kahlua0.7 shot, Espresso Coffee1-2 shots, Sugar Syrup 0.3 shot

Combine all ingredients in a shaker filled with ice & shake well. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail Martini glass. Carefully place three coffee beans on top of the drink for garnish & serve.

Spirit Measure Martini Glass Shaker
